    Easy to create

    Domain included

    Select an annual plan to receive a free associated domain, including renewal costs, for as long as your subscription is active. Additional domain extension options are also available at full price.

    Includes business mail

    Carry over the professional image to each email you send. Your customers and partners will easily recognize you@yourbusiness.com.

    $100 Credit

    Search credit is valid in the US only and requires creation of separate Bing Network account. Cannot be combined with any other offer or discount, separated, redeemed for cash, transferred, sold, or bartered.

    Satisfaction guarantee

    You have the assurance of our 30-day satisfaction guarantee. You shouldn't need it, but it's good to know it's there.
    30-day Satisfaction Guarantee

    The 30-day Satisfaction Guarantee applies to new orders. If you are not completely satisfied, you may receive a refund of your Website Builder subscription fee, less $9.95 for domain name registration, if included in your plan. You may keep the domain name, if you request to do so at the time of cancellation, which must be within 30 calendar days of the date of your order. Your domain name may then be transferred to a new registrar after 60 days. All refund requests must be made by calling 1-866-781-9246 and must be specifically requested at the time of cancellation. Refunds will be made only to the Account Owner and only to the credit card on file. Refunds are available for Website Builder only and not for additional services purchased. See the Terms of Service for additional terms and conditions.

    24/7 support

    Just in case you have an issue, you can reach Customer Care around the clock with questions about your site.

    Live Insights

    Live Insights lets you stay on on top of key site metrics, view visitors in real time, and even initiate a chat with customers.

    Privacy protection

    All domain owners and their contact information are listed publicly online. We protect your identity with private domain registration.
    Private Domain Registration

    Private Domain Registration is included in the Professional Plan. It can be added to the Basic Plan for $.99/mo.
